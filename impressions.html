Impressions of the Elite

"He weaves a tapestry of ethereal beauty and embraces his audience in the eternal spirit of India. At every step in the Audio-Visual Pilgrimage with Shri C.V. Gopinath, we find the truth; the good, the beautiful and the splendor created by the Man and Nature. Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru's Discovery of India left a lasting impression on my generation. The Audio-Visual presentation on 'Pilgrimage to Kailas and Manassarovar' of C.V.Gopinath is somewhat like Panditji's Discovery of India."

Late Dr. L.M. Singhvi

Eminent Jurist & Formerly Indian High Commissioner to U.K.

"Shri C.V. Gopinath, with his erudite scholarship and devotion to revive roots of our great cultural heritage is a natural cultural Ambassador of Karnataka."

T.N. Chaturvedi

Governor, Karnataka

"Shri C.V. Gopinath's audio-visual presentation on Himalayas in Vedic Concepts is something very rare to see. The spiritual fervour that he creates and above all his passion to tell people about Himalayas and its vedic culture are very impressive. His narration and interluding references to Karnataka Culture and the vedic practices and its roots in Karnataka should make one proud that he belongs to State of Karnataka, though he has been a resident of Delhi for more than two decades."

Tarun Gogoi

Chief Minister, Assam

"Although we spent the time inside a dark room, it was possible to feel a strange light beginning to glimmer inside the inner self… our deep gratitude to you for enabling us to undergo such a moving and enlightening experience."

Bharat Vyas

Asstt. Resident Representative (Programme), UNDP, India

"It is heartening to learn that, after a distinguished career in the civil services, you have devoted yourself to guiding and mentoring civil services aspirants in the areas of ethics and moral leadership. Such efforts make a meaningful contribution to nurturing a generation of conscientious public servants. I am also pleased to know about your remarkable initiative, 'OM – The Primordial Sound,' conceived, scripted and recited by you on the wisdom of the Vedas and Upanishads."

Dr. Himanta Biswa Sarma

Chief Minister of Assam · Letter dated 7th June, 2026

N. Vittal

Central Vigilance Commissioner

"I had the privilege of viewing the video cassettes of Shri Gopinath's Pilgrimage to Kailash Parbat. Throughout the viewing, we forgot the world around us and were so engrossed that we did not know when the time passed. It was an elevating experience and we hope it had lasted longer."

Justice P.B. Sawant

Chairman, Press Council of India

"Shri Gopinath's lucid commentary and his own impressive and chaste Vedic recitations from the background of the presentation which also contains excellent musical rendering taken from great devotional texts. On the whole, one sits deeply engrossed if not spell bound throughout the presentation… I hope he will bring out many more such presentations on other great centers of Indian Spiritual Heritage."

Bhure Lal

Secretary (Co-ord. & Plg.), Cabinet Secretariat

It is remarkable that the audience which constituted largely of restless teenagers, whose span of attention does not usually exceed a few minutes, was held spell bound for over 2 hours."
